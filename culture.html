By Rabindranath Tagore

In India's heart, a melody so sweet,

Where diverse cultures joyfully meet.

From north to south, and east to west,

Our traditions thrive, forever blessed.


With vibrant colors and rhythms grand,

Our dances tell stories of our land.

In temples old, our prayers ascend,

A timeless bond that will never end.


Through art and poetry, we express,

The soul of India, in its finesse.

In every smile and every tear,

Our culture's beauty shines so clear.

RISHIKAA MODI
